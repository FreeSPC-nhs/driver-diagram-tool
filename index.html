Driver Diagram Tool

Early version of a web-based driver diagram tool. Add an aim, primary/secondary drivers and change ideas, download them as CSV, and re-upload later to carry on. Below is a first graphical view similar in spirit to LIFE QI.

Typically the aim is at the centre, primary drivers connect to the aim, secondary drivers to a primary driver, and change ideas to a secondary (or primary) driver.

Use CSV to save your driver diagram and reload it later.
