Driver Diagram Tool

This is an early version of a web-based driver diagram tool. You can add an aim, primary/secondary drivers and change ideas, download them as a CSV file, and re-upload the CSV later to carry on where you left off. The visual diagram and export as image/PDF will come next.

1. Add items

For most diagrams, the aim is at the centre, primary drivers connect to the aim, secondary drivers to a primary driver, and change ideas to a secondary (or primary) driver. You can change these later.

2. Import / export

Use CSV to save your driver diagram and reload it another time.
